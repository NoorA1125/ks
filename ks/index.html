About Us

Personal shopping, private styling, personal and corporate image consulting based in Atlanta & by Kevin Samuels is an image consulting company providing services such as image and brand integration, wardrobe styling, and media and job interview coaching for clients including recording artists, entrepreneurs, job seekers, and anyone wanting to improve their image.

Today the world is ultra competitive, hyper connected, and more image conscience than ever. Within the first few seconds of meeting someone new they've formed a lasting impression of you. We help you take charge of that knowledge and turn into your opportunity.

Passion for helping everyday people look and feel their best without being a hassle is why we do what we do. And the best part is we make it simple and easy. You don't have to know all of the best looks just what looks best on you.
